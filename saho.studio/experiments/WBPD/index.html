RCB X NIKELAB
Design Consultant, 2018

I worked with a small creative team for Rochambeau remotely to help produce early concepts and digital representations of the shoes. Due to collaborative nature of this projnnect, I needed to move quickly to visually interpret napkin drawings and phone calls into thumbnails, presentable concepts, and CAD drawings. I worked primarily in Solidworks and prepared the files to send to the 3D printers. Having worked with the brand before, I was already familiar with their brand identity and their year long partnership with artist, Aaron Curry.

This collaboration between Rochambeau and NIKELAB is still under production, but view the pre-release here.
