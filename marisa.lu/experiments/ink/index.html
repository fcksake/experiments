WHAT IS IT?
A platform game with additional features like drawing in 3D, and switching perspectives.
IDEA
Toxic rain has washed the ink from Mo's world. Help our brush tipped hero recollect ink to refill his home! Avoid puddle monsters, and with the extra ink, draw platforms as needed to help get from place to place.
EXECUTION
Animations of characters were all hand drawn. Game scripts written in C#. Landscape features either made in SketchUp or downloaded.
  • press [enter] or [return] to change perspective/movement axis
  • Use your painting ability to help get to hard to reach places!
  • Hand drawn character animation
  • The toxic rain will wash you away! Careful!
